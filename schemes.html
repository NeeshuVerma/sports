Schemes / Programmes

Khelo Haryana Scheme

Promotes sports at grassroots level and identifies young talent across the state.

Sports Nursery Scheme

Training young athletes in various sports through structured coaching programs.

Mission Olympics Programme

Prepares elite athletes for international events including Olympics and Asian Games.

Sports Infrastructure Development

Development of stadiums, sports complexes, and training centres in districts.

Coach Development Programme

Upgrading coaching standards through workshops and certification courses.

Providing scientific fitness training and diet planning to athletes.
